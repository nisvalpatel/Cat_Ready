# CAT Vehicle Inspection Checklist  
_Based on Daily Safety & Maintenance Report_

## Vehicle Covered

- Equipment Family: Medium Wheel Loader  
- Make: Caterpillar  
- Model: 982  

---

## Inspection Categories & Checklist

---

## A. FROM THE GROUND (Exterior Walkaround)

### Tires & Undercarriage
- Inspect tires for wear, cuts, pressure
- Inspect rims for cracks or damage
- Inspect underneath of machine
- Inspect axles
- Inspect final drives
- Inspect differentials
- Inspect brakes
- Inspect duo-cone seals

### Bucket & Lift System
- Inspect bucket cutting edge / tips
- Inspect bucket tilt cylinders & hoses
- Inspect lift cylinders & hoses
- Inspect lift arm attachment to frame

### Drivetrain
- Inspect transmission & transfer gears
- Check differential & final drive oil levels
- Check transmission oil level & condition

### Tanks & Fluid Systems
- Inspect fuel tank for leaks/damage
- Inspect hydraulic fluid tank
- Inspect brake air tank

### Safety & Access
- Inspect steps and handrails
- Test work lights
- Inspect battery & cables

---

## B. ENGINE COMPARTMENT

### Fluid Levels
- Check engine oil level
- Check engine coolant level
- Check transmission oil
- Check hydraulic fluid level

### Cooling System
- Inspect radiator cores for debris
- Inspect coolant condition

### Fuel System
- Inspect primary fuel filter
- Inspect secondary fuel filter

### Air Intake
- Inspect air cleaner
- Check air filter service indicator

### Belts & Hoses
- Inspect hoses for cracks or leaks
- Inspect belts for wear and tension

### Overall Condition
- General engine compartment inspection

---

## C. OUTSIDE THE CAB

- Inspect ROPS/FOPS structure
- Verify fire extinguisher is present and charged
- Inspect steps & handrails
- Inspect side doors
- Test windshield wipers & washers

---

## D. INSIDE THE CAB

### Operator Safety
- Inspect seat condition
- Inspect seat belt & mounting
- Test horn
- Test backup alarm

### Visibility
- Inspect windows
- Inspect mirrors

### Controls & Monitoring
- Test indicators & gauges
- Test switch functionality
- Verify monitor display operation

---

## Vehicles This Checklist Applies To

- Medium Wheel Loaders
- Large Wheel Loaders
- Front-End Loaders
- Quarry Loaders
- Mining Loaders
- Heavy Construction Earthmoving Equipment
